Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

Introduction

Swaram ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website at ecoliving-tips.github.io. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our website to browse Malayalam Christian song chord charts.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Song Request Data: When you submit a song request, we collect your name, the song title, and any optional message you provide. This is stored in our database to help us prioritize which songs to add.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Like most websites, we automatically receive technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. This helps us understand how our site is used and improve it.
  • Cookies: We use cookies through Google AdSense and YouTube embeds. These are explained in detail below.

Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free availability of our chord charts. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the Internet. Specifically:

  • Google uses the DART cookie to serve ads based on your browsing history.
  • Google may collect information about your device, browser, IP address, and activity on our site.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies at aboutads.info.

YouTube Integration

Some of our song pages include embedded YouTube videos for tutorial purposes. When you play these videos, YouTube (Google) may collect data about your viewing behavior according to their own privacy policy. We embed videos using YouTube's standard embed code. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy for details on how YouTube handles your data.

Supabase (Song Requests)

Song requests submitted through our form are stored using Supabase, a cloud database service. We only store the information you explicitly provide: your name, the song title, and your optional message. We do not collect email addresses or any other personal information through the request form.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display and improve our website and chord charts;
  • To process your song requests and prioritize which songs to add;
  • To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense;
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns so we can improve the user experience;
  • To comply with legal obligations if required.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Google: Through AdSense for advertising and through YouTube for embedded video functionality.
  • Supabase: For storing song request data.
  • Law enforcement: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized ads through your Google account settings;
  • Block cookies through your browser settings;
  • Request deletion of any song request data you have submitted by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through our song request form or by visiting our website.
