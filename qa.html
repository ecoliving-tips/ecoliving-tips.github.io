Can you recommend good digital keyboards for beginners?

Equipment Advice January 10, 2025 Asked by Maria

I want to start learning Malayalam Christian songs but don't have a keyboard yet. What features should I look for and what's a good budget range?

Expert Answer:

Great question, Maria! Here are my recommendations for beginners:

Essential Features:

  • 61 keys minimum (88 preferred if budget allows)
  • Touch sensitivity (velocity sensitive keys)
  • Built-in speakers
  • Headphone jack for practice
  • Basic piano and organ sounds

Budget-Friendly Options:

  • ₹15,000-25,000: Yamaha PSR-E373, Casio CT-X700
  • ₹25,000-40,000: Yamaha PSR-EW310, Roland E-X20
  • ₹40,000+: Yamaha PSR-E463, Casio CT-X5000

Remember: the best keyboard is the one you'll actually use! Start with your budget and upgrade later as you progress.
