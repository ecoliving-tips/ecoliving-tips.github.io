About Swaram

Swaram is a free resource built for the Malayalam Christian music community. We believe every church musician, worship leader, and music enthusiast should have easy access to accurate chord charts without paying for expensive sheet music.

Our collection focuses on Syro Malabar Holy Mass songs, traditional Malayalam Christian hymns, Marian devotional songs, and contemporary praise & worship music. Whether you play keyboard at your parish, strum guitar at a prayer group, or simply want to learn your favorite devotional songs at home, our chord charts are designed to help you play with confidence.

We are constantly growing our library based on what our community asks for. Every chord chart is carefully written to be accurate and beginner-friendly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I read the chord notation?

Our chords use standard Western music notation. Capital letters like C, G, D represent major chords. A lowercase "m" means minor (e.g., Am = A minor). Numbers indicate extended chords like G7 (dominant seventh) or Gsus4 (suspended fourth). The chords are arranged in sequence showing when to change.

Can I request a specific song?

Yes! Visit our Request Song page and fill in the song title. We prioritize the most requested songs from our community, especially popular Syro Malabar Holy Mass songs and Malayalam praise & worship songs.

Are these chords suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our chord charts are designed to be beginner-friendly. Most songs use standard chords like C, G, Am, and Em with clear progression patterns that are easy to follow on both keyboard and guitar.

Do you have Syro Malabar Holy Mass song chords?

Yes, we specialize in Syro Malabar Holy Mass song chords. Our collection includes traditional liturgical songs, communion hymns, and songs commonly sung during the Syro Malabar Rite. We add more songs regularly based on community requests.
