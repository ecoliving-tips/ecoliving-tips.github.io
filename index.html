Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reduce my carbon footprint?

Reduce your carbon footprint by using energy-efficient appliances, reducing meat consumption, minimizing single-use plastics, choosing public transportation or carpooling, and supporting renewable energy sources.

What are the benefits of sustainable living?

Sustainable living helps preserve natural resources, reduces pollution and waste, saves money through lower energy consumption, improves your health through cleaner air and water, and contributes to building resilient communities.

How can I start composting at home?

Begin by selecting a compost bin or designating an area in your yard. Collect kitchen scraps (fruits, vegetables, coffee grounds, eggshells) and yard waste (leaves, grass clippings). Layer 'browns' (dry materials) with 'greens' (food scraps), turn regularly, and maintain moisture like a wrung-out sponge.

What are the best eco-friendly alternatives to plastic?

Great alternatives include glass containers for food storage, stainless steel or bamboo straws, beeswax wraps instead of plastic wrap, cloth shopping bags, bamboo toothbrushes, silicone food storage bags, and natural fiber clothing.

How can I save energy at home?

Install LED bulbs, use smart power strips, upgrade to energy-efficient appliances, improve insulation, use programmable thermostats, wash clothes in cold water, air-dry when possible, and unplug electronics when not in use.
